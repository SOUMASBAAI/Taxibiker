# France Regulatory Bundle Setup Guide

## üá´üá∑ Understanding France Regulatory Requirements

When you purchase a French phone number (+33), France requires **regulatory compliance** to prove you're authorized to use it for business purposes.

## üìã What is a Regulatory Bundle?

A **Regulatory Bundle** is a collection of documents and information that proves:

- Your identity
- Your right to use a French phone number
- Your business legitimacy
- Compliance with French telecom regulations

## üîç Required Information for "France Local Individual"

### Personal Information:

```
- Full Legal Name (as on ID documents)
- Date of Birth
- Address in France (residential or business)
- Phone Number (for verification)
- Email Address
```

### Required Documents:

```
1. Government-issued Photo ID
   - French National ID Card, OR
   - EU Passport, OR
   - French Driver's License

2. Proof of Address (dated within 3 months)
   - Utility bill (electricity, gas, water)
   - Bank statement
   - Lease agreement
   - Property tax statement

3. Business Documentation (for TaxiBiker)
   - Business registration certificate
   - SIRET number documentation
   - Proof of business address
```

## üöÄ Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Create Regulatory Bundle

1. **In Twilio Console**

   - Go to **Develop** ‚Üí **Regulatory Compliance** ‚Üí **Bundles**
   - Click **"Create a Regulatory Bundle"**

2. **Select Bundle Type**

   - Choose **"France Local Individual"**
   - This is for individual business owners

3. **Fill Required Information**
   ```
   Bundle Name: TaxiBiker France Compliance
   End User Type: Individual
   Country: France
   Regulation: France Local Individual
   ```

### Step 2: Upload Documents

1. **Identity Document**

   - Upload clear photo/scan of your ID
   - Ensure all text is readable
   - File formats: PDF, JPG, PNG

2. **Proof of Address**

   - Upload recent utility bill or bank statement
   - Must show your name and French address
   - Dated within last 3 months

3. **Business Documents**
   - Business registration
   - SIRET documentation
   - Any other business proof

### Step 3: Submit for Review

1. **Review Information**

   - Double-check all details are correct
   - Ensure documents are clear and readable

2. **Submit Bundle**

   - Click "Submit for Review"
   - Review typically takes 1-3 business days

3. **Wait for Approval**
   - Twilio will review your documents
   - You'll receive email notification when approved

### Step 4: Assign Bundle to Number

1. **After Approval**

   - Go back to your phone number settings
   - Click "Assign approved Bundle"
   - Select your approved bundle

2. **Complete Assignment**
   - Your number is now compliant
   - You can proceed with WhatsApp setup

## üö® Important Notes

### Document Requirements:

- **High quality scans/photos** (all text must be readable)
- **Recent documents** (proof of address within 3 months)
- **Matching names** (all documents must show same name)
- **French address** (required for French numbers)

### Processing Time:

- **Bundle creation**: 5-10 minutes
- **Document review**: 1-3 business days
- **Bundle assignment**: Immediate after approval

### Common Issues:

- **Blurry documents** ‚Üí Re-upload clearer versions
- **Old proof of address** ‚Üí Use document within 3 months
- **Name mismatch** ‚Üí Ensure all documents show same name
- **Missing business docs** ‚Üí Add business registration
